Introduction

Essay writing is a critical component of the UPSC Mains exam that tests your ability to present coherent, balanced, and structured arguments. This guide provides tips, suggestions, and valuable video resources to improve your essay writing skills.

Top Tips for Essay Writing

  • Understand the topic carefully before starting your essay.
  • Create an outline to organize your thoughts and arguments.
  • Use a clear structure: Introduction, Body, Conclusion.
  • Support your points with facts, examples, and balanced arguments.
  • Maintain coherence and avoid repetition.
  • Practice time management to complete your essay within the exam time.
  • Keep language simple, precise, and impactful.
  • Revise your essay for grammar and spelling errors if time permits.
  • Read a variety of essays to get exposure to different styles.
  • Focus on your own voice and viewpoint while keeping it respectful and inclusive.
