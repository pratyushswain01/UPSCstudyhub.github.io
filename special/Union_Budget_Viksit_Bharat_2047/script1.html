National Youth Parliament Master Script

The Yuva Shakti Manifesto

I. The Exordium: The Civilizational Call

(Rise calmly, adjust the microphone, pause for two seconds, and address the Chair.) “उत्तिष्ठत जाग्रत प्राप्य वरान्निबोधत।” Arise, awake, and stop not till the goal is achieved — Katha Upanishad Respected Chairperson, Honourable Members of this august house, आज की चर्चा केवल बजट की नहीं है; आज की चर्चा भारत के भविष्य की है। इसलिए मैं इस सदन से विनम्रतापूर्वक कहना चाहूँगा — अपनी कुर्सी की पेटी बाँध लीजिए, क्योंकि हम एक ऐसी यात्रा पर चर्चा करने जा रहे हैं जो हमें सीधे विकसित भारत 2047 की ओर ले जाती है।

The Vision of the Budget

I stand here not to just explain some financial paper. This Union Budget for 2026-27 is bigger than that. Its like a plan connecting our young people to this big dream of a developed India. Every budget shows what a country really cares about. What it values, who it helps, and the kind of future it wants to make happen. And right now, that future depends on Indias youth power, the Yuva Shakti. We are the Amrit Peedhi, the generation that gets to see India go from developing to a top player in the world economy and tech. It seems kind of exciting when you think about it. Chairperson, some people look at education just through literacy stats. But this government sees it differently, more about jobs, new ideas, and how it boosts the whole country. Thats why theres this huge allocation, 1.39 lakh crore rupees to the Ministry of Education. Its historic, I think. Money by itself doesnt change everything though. You need a real vision too. We set up this EEE Committee for Education, Employment, and Enterprise. The point is to make sure college degrees lead to real work in the digital world, not just be pieces of paper. Were also spreading out creative stuff with 15,500 AVGC Content Creator Labs in schools and colleges everywhere. So maybe the next big game maker or AI expert comes from places like Surat or Sambalpur, not only fancy spots abroad. That changes things. Through all this, were telling young people in India that their ideas arent just fun. Theyre part of the economy now. A national asset, sort of.

Inclusive Growth

No country gets to Viksit Bharat if chances arent equal for everyone. Growth has to be quick but also fair. This budget pushes hard on including people and making things accessible. Were putting a girls hostel in every district. So distance wont stop anyone from studying. Also, the tax on loans for studying overseas is down to 2 percent. That means global chances open up for talent, not just for those with money. A farmers daughter can aim high, same as some big business kids son. Thats what Sabka Saath, Sabka Vikas, Sabka Vishwas really means, I guess.

Innovation & Entrepreneurship

Indias youth arent waiting for jobs. Theyre making them, building the future. The 10,000 crore SME Growth Fund helps young business starters and new companies. Then theres Semiconductor Mission 2.0, getting India into making chips for the world. Whoever leads in tech now will run the global economy later. Our aim is to shift from just using innovations to creating them everywhere. From looking for jobs to making them. From basic skills to leading in knowledge. It feels like sometimes people miss how connected this all is.

Vision of Viksit Bharat

From a startups quiet code dreams to rushing into solar and hydrogen energy. With AI guiding and digital everything, Viksit Bharat is the one goal. Hostels for girls in every town, breaking down barriers. Labs for creators up to chip factories, opening that 2047 path wide. Bits of tech code mixed with green fields. A smarter India starts showing up. Hearts and minds geared up. We shape Viksit Bharat that way. Budgets each year make the road clearer. Not just separate numbers. A map to that developed India.

The Closing Statement

Chairperson, history should note that in 2026 we didnt just balance money. We balanced chances for people. This empowers students and idea makers, business starters, dreamers. Mostly, it lifts up Indias youth. The road to Viksit Bharat 2047 isnt only in papers. Its in classes, labs, new ventures, villages, and that drive from young people that wont stop. Viksit Bharat isnt some government thing alone. Its a movement for everyone, a Jan Andolan. The starting point is set. Youth energy is fueling it. Indias tomorrow is launching. Let us go up together. Jai Hind. Jai Bharat.
