Introduction

The General Studies papers in UPSC Mains cover a wide range of topics to test candidates' knowledge, analytical skills, and ability to apply concepts for governance and society. This page provides a complete syllabus, study materials, tips, and recommended books to help you prepare effectively.

Complete GS Mains Syllabus

  • GS Paper 1: Indian Heritage and Culture; History of Modern India; Geography of India and the World
  • GS Paper 2: Governance, Constitution, Polity, Social Justice and International Relations
  • GS Paper 3: Technology, Economic Development, Biodiversity, Environment, Security and Disaster Management
  • GS Paper 4: Ethics, Integrity, and Aptitude including Case Studies

Study Contents & Focus Areas

  • GS Paper 1: Ancient and Medieval Indian Culture, Freedom Struggle, Post-Independence Indian History, Indian and World Geography.
  • GS Paper 2: Indian Constitution and Political System, Governance & Public Policy, Social Justice, International Relations and Institutions.
  • GS Paper 3: Economic Development and Planning, Agriculture, Science & Technology, Environment & Ecology, Security Issues and Disaster Management.
  • GS Paper 4: Ethics and Human Interface, Attitude, Emotional Intelligence, Probity in Governance, Case Studies.

Preparation Tips

  • Understand the syllabus in-depth before starting preparation.
  • Read NCERT books thoroughly for basics.
  • Regularly follow a good current affairs source like The Hindu or PIB.
  • Practice answer writing for clarity and time management.
  • Make concise notes for revision.
  • Focus on case studies and ethical dilemmas for GS4.
  • Revise regularly and attempt previous year question papers.

Recommended Books & Authors

  • Indian Polity - M. Laxmikanth
  • Indian Art and Culture - Nitin Singhania
  • Modern India - Bipin Chandra
  • Indian Economy - Ramesh Singh
  • Certificate Physical and Human Geography - G.C. Leong
  • Ethics, Integrity & Aptitude - Subba Rao & P.N. Roy Chowdhury
  • Environment and Ecology - Shankar IAS Environment Book
