About the Exam

The Entrance Test is designed to evaluate your knowledge, skills, and preparedness for the program. Below are the key details:

  • Test Name: Entrance Test
  • Duration: 3 Hours
  • Syllabus: Quantitative Aptitude, Logical Reasoning, General Knowledge, and English.
  • Marking Scheme: Each correct answer awards 4 marks, and each incorrect answer deducts 1 mark.
  • Required Materials: Hall ticket, valid ID, and necessary stationery.

Important Instructions

Please ensure you follow these guidelines during the exam:

  • Reach the exam center at least 30 minutes prior to the start time.
  • Mobile phones and electronic devices are strictly prohibited.
  • Carry your original ID proof and the printed admit card.
  • Use only black or blue pens for answering the questions.
