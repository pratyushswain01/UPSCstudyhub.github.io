About UPSC Hub

An integrated platform to develop all the skills needed for UPSC preparation. UPSC Hub was created and designed by Pratyush Swain with the vision to provide comprehensive resources for UPSC aspirants.

Our mission is to simplify UPSC preparation by offering structured courses, mock tests, study materials, and revision tools - all in one place.

Our Story

UPSC Hub was born out of the struggles faced by countless UPSC aspirants who found it challenging to access quality resources in one place. Recognizing the need for a centralized platform that could provide comprehensive preparation materials, we set out to create UPSC Hub.

What started as a small initiative has now grown into a trusted resource for thousands of civil service aspirants across the country. Our platform continues to evolve based on user feedback and the changing patterns of the UPSC examination.
