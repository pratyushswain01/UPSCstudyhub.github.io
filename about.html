The Vision

UPSChub is a premier learning ecosystem designed to modernize and streamline the preparation process for the Civil Services Examination. The platform bridges the gap between traditional study methods and the digital-first requirements of today's competitive landscape.

Meet the Founder

Pratyush Swain is a tech enthusiast with a deep-rooted passion for Physics and Artificial Intelligence. With a background in B.Sc. Physics from Berhampur University (SDVM), he brings a disciplined, analytical approach to UPSC preparation.

Based in Berhampur, Odisha, Pratyush is dedicated to empowering the next generation of IPS and IAS officers through innovative educational strategies.

AI Specialization

AI-Driven Content Curation Refining daily current affairs for maximum relevance and exam-specific focus.
Smart Analytics Helping students track their progress through data-driven insights and pattern recognition.
Modern Pedagogy Combining disciplined study plans with refined learning technology and AI tools.
